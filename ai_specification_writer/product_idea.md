# Product Idea

## Product Name
**StudySync**

## Vision

StudySync is an AI-powered learning platform designed to help students organize their studies, collaborate with classmates, and improve academic performance through personalized learning tools and smart study planning.

## Primary Users

### Students
**Needs:**
- Organize assignments and deadlines.
- Create effective study schedules.
- Access study materials in one place.
- Collaborate with classmates on projects.

### Teachers
**Needs:**
- Share learning resources efficiently.
- Monitor student progress.
- Create assignments and quizzes.
- Communicate with students easily.

### Educational Institutions
**Needs:**
- Improve student engagement.
- Track academic performance.
- Provide a centralized learning platform.
- Support both in-person and remote learning.

## Key Features

- **AI Study Planner**
  - Generates personalized study schedules based on deadlines, workload, and learning goals.

- **Assignment Tracker**
  - Helps students manage homework, projects, and exam dates with reminders.

- **Smart Notes**
  - Organizes notes and generates summaries and flashcards using AI.

- **Collaborative Study Groups**
  - Enables students to create study groups, share files, and communicate in real time.

- **Practice Quizzes**
  - Generates quizzes from uploaded notes and course materials.

- **Progress Dashboard**
  - Displays learning progress, completed tasks, and upcoming deadlines.

- **Calendar Integration**
  - Synchronizes study plans with personal calendars.

## Differentiators

- AI-generated personalized study plans that adapt to each student's progress.
- Automatic quiz and flashcard generation from uploaded documents.
- Real-time collaboration tools for group learning.
- Learning analytics that identify weak topics and recommend additional practice.
- Cross-platform access on web and mobile devices.
