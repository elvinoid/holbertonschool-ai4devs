# UI Concept – StudySync

## Purpose

StudySync is an AI-powered learning platform designed to help students organize their studies, improve productivity, and enhance learning through artificial intelligence. The application provides tools for study planning, assignment management, AI-generated learning materials, and progress tracking in a simple and user-friendly interface.

---

## Users

### Students
- Create and manage study schedules.
- Upload learning materials.
- Generate AI summaries, flashcards, and quizzes.
- Track study progress and assignment deadlines.

### Teachers
- Upload course materials.
- Create assignments and quizzes.
- Monitor student submissions and performance.
- Share announcements with students.

### Administrators
- Manage user accounts and permissions.
- Monitor system activity.
- Generate reports and analytics.
- Configure platform settings.

---

## Core Screens

### Login & Registration
- Secure user authentication.
- Account registration and password recovery.

### Dashboard
- Overview of upcoming study sessions.
- Assignment deadlines.
- Recent learning activity.
- Progress summary and notifications.

### Study Planner
- Calendar view of study sessions.
- Create, edit, and delete study schedules.
- Daily and weekly planning.

### Study Materials
- Upload notes, presentations, and documents.
- Organize materials by subject.
- Search and filter learning resources.

### AI Learning Assistant
- Generate summaries from uploaded documents.
- Create flashcards and quizzes.
- Ask AI questions about study materials.

### Assignments
- View assigned coursework.
- Submit completed assignments.
- Track assignment status and grades.

### Progress Dashboard
- View completed study sessions.
- Monitor quiz scores and learning statistics.
- Track progress toward study goals.

### Profile & Settings
- Update personal information.
- Change password.
- Configure notification preferences.
- Manage account settings.

---

## Actions

- Register a new account.
- Log in and log out securely.
- Create, edit, and delete study sessions.
- Upload and organize study materials.
- Generate AI summaries, flashcards, and quizzes.
- View and submit assignments.
- Monitor learning progress and quiz results.
- Receive notifications for deadlines and reminders.
- Update profile information and application settings.
